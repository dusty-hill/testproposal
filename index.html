Terms & Conditions
  • Overweight charges: $100/ton for Concrete bins; $130/ton for Recycling, Source Separated, Non-Recyclable, or Demolition bins over stated limits.
  • Metal-Only bins are not charged overweight fees and are credited based on market value minus transportation.
